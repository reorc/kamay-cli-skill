# Market Analyst

## Introduction

Market Analyst is a specialized capability focused on e-commerce category research, transforming complex market data into actionable marketing insights. It automatically invokes data tools from platforms like Amazon and TikTok to deeply analyze category trends, consumer demand, product reviews, and competitive positioning.

## Use Cases

Use Market Analyst when you need to understand:

- **Category Opportunity Assessment**: Is a category worth entering? What's the growth potential?
- **Consumer Demand Analysis**: What are the pain points and purchase drivers of target users?
- **Competitive Analysis**: What are competitors' strengths and weaknesses? How to differentiate?
- **Product Review Analysis (VOC)**: What do users really think about products? What improvement directions exist?
- **Seasonality Trends**: When is the peak sales season? How to time promotions?
- **Pricing Strategy**: What's the market price distribution? What pricing opportunities exist?

## Core Capabilities

### Category Trend Analysis
- Identify category growth trajectories and emerging sub-categories
- Discover market saturation signals and blue ocean opportunities
- Analyze category competitive landscape and top players

### Consumer Demand Insights
- Extract user pain points and unmet needs
- Identify purchase triggers and decision key points
- Analyze user profiles and behavioral characteristics

### Product VOC Analysis
- Review sentiment analysis (positive/negative/neutral)
- Extract recurring complaints and praise points
- Identify star rating drivers
- Conduct competitive comparison from buyer perspective

### Seasonality Pattern Recognition
- Identify sales peaks and troughs
- Recommend promotional windows and content timing
- Analyze historical trends to predict future patterns

### Positioning Opportunity Discovery
- Discover price gaps and market niches
- Identify underserved segments
- Provide differentiation positioning recommendations

## How to Use

### Basic Usage

Simply ask Kamay market analysis questions, and the system will automatically invoke the Market Analyst:

```
Analyze the wireless earbuds category on Amazon US, help me understand market opportunities
```

```
Help me analyze user reviews for this product, identify main pain points and selling points
```

```
Compare and analyze user feedback differences between my product and competitors
```

### Analysis Workflow

1. **Ask Question**: Describe the market or product question you want to understand
2. **Data Collection**: System automatically invokes relevant platform APIs to collect data
3. **Insight Generation**: Analyze data and output marketing-oriented conclusions
4. **Report Output**: Generate structured market insight report

### Output Format

Market Analyst generates reports containing:

- **Executive Summary**: 3-5 key findings impacting marketing decisions
- **Insights Analysis**: Category trends, consumer demand, VOC, seasonality, positioning opportunities
- **Supporting Data**: Comparison tables, key metrics (bold), trend arrows (↑↓→)
- **Action Items**: Prioritized specific recommendations with expected impact

## Usage Tips

### Tips to Improve Analysis Quality

1. **Specify Analysis Scope**: Indicate platform (Amazon/TikTok), market (US/UK/JP, etc.), category
2. **Provide Product Information**: For VOC analysis, provide ASIN or product link
3. **Explain Analysis Purpose**: Tell the analyst your decision context (new product development? pricing strategy? ad optimization?)

### Example Requests

**Category Analysis**:
```
Analyze the skincare category on Amazon US, focusing on the anti-aging segment,
I want to understand market opportunities and consumer demand
```

**VOC Analysis**:
```
Analyze user reviews for ASIN B09XXXXXX, help me find product improvement directions and ad selling points
```

**Competitive Comparison**:
```
Compare user reviews for these three products: B09XXXXXX, B08YYYYY, B07ZZZZZZ,
analyze their respective strengths and weaknesses
```
